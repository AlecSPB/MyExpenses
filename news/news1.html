New in 1.5.0.: Payment methods

Through this new channel, I will publish regularly explanations and discussions of My Expenses's functionality. In contrast to the tutorial, which I try to keep consistent with the current release, and which is translated to all supported languages, these articles are meant to document new features, as they arise, to generate comments from users and hence to contribute to the app's continuos improvement.

In answer to requests voiced already by its first users, version 1.5.0. of My Expenses introduced payment methods. The functionality is equivalent to how payment methods work in Grisbi: Payment methods are linked to accounts through account types. When you create an account, you define its type as one of CASH, BANK, ASSET or LIABILITY. When you create transactions in a given account, you can then make use of the payment methods that are linked to its account type.

Through the "Manage payment methods" activity from the preferences screen, you can change, add and delete methods.

My Expenses has four predefined payment methods, all of them by default linked only to bank accounts: CHEQUE, CREDITCARD, DEPOSIT, DIRECTDEBIT. Those cannot be deleted, or renamed, but you can alter their association with account types. Clicking on a method or activating the New button brings you to the following screen:

Here you decide if the method should only be used for expenses (credit), incomes (debit), or both (neutral). And you check the account types where you want to make use of it. Now in the edit screen for transactions you will see a line labeled Method, with a button opening the dialog where you select one of the applicable methods for the transaction.

Unfortunately, payment methods are not yet expressed in the QIF export, because the standard does not provide a field for this information. If you have an idea on how to export transactions in a more expressive format, let me know.
