My Expenses News

2012-11-22

New feature in upcoming 1.6.0 release: Templates

My Expenses now tries to help you enter recurrent transactions: say you often eat a fixed-priced meal in your favourite restaurant, or transfer the same amount of money from one account to the other, or buy the weekly magazine at your newsstand, you name it. After you have entered the transaction once, you call the Create template command from its context menu:

Enter a title for the template:

Templates are available through the New button's popup window.

Clicking on a template's title results in the instantiation of a new transaction with all information (amount, category, comment, payee, payment method) from the template. Only time and date reflect the current moment.

In the same way you can define templates for transfers. Templates are linked to accounts, so you only you see templates defined for the current account in the popup menu.

Inspecting the list of templates and deleting the ones you no longer need, is available from the preferences.

Beta 1 of 1.6.0 is available from Github's Downloads and shortly through F-Droid.
