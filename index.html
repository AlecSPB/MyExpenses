My Expenses is an Android application designed to keep track of your expenses and incomes, and to export them as QIF files into a desktop finance tool, like Grisbi or Gnucash.
Requires Android 2.0.

Features:

  • multiple accounts with transfers.
  • two levels of categories (import from Grisbi XML)
  • upload QIF file via FTP or email
  • data backup and restore

https://market.android.com/details?id=org.totschnig.myexpenses
