My Expenses is free software. It has been started for a personal need and out of curiosity for the Android platform. I am happy, if you find it useful. Period.

For those who want to give something back, here are some ways to do that:

Voice
Working on My Expenses is fun and rewarding through your appreciative and constructive feedback. Leave a comment in the Market, or get in contact.
Ideas
My Expenses evolves through your reports and suggestions. If you find a problem or think of an improvement, create a new issue, in Github's Issue Tracker, or get in contact.
Time
If you are interested in translating My Expenses into a language you speak, in testing beta releases, get in contact.
Money
You can use the Flattr or Paypal buttons in the footer. If you consider sponsoring a specific new feature, get in contact.
