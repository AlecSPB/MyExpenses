If you came here, because you find My Expenses useful, and thought there might be something you could do to support its continued improvement: Great, please read on! Any of the following contributions will make My Expenses better:

Voice
Working on My Expenses is fun and rewarding through your appreciative and constructive feedback. Leave a comment in Google Play, or add your comment to one of the posts in the news section.
Ideas
My Expenses evolves through your reports and suggestions. If you find a problem or think of an improvement, create a new issue, in Github's Issue Tracker.
Time
Help translate My Expenses into a language you speak, test a beta release. Of course you can also hack on the source code.
Money
Flattr or send money via PayPal
