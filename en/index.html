Summary

  • Keep track of your expenses and incomes.
  • Manage them directly on your smartphone.
  • Export them into a Desktop finance program like Grisbi (Open Source), Gnucash (Open Source), Quicken or MS Money.
  • Works on all Android versions from 2.1. to 4.3.

Features

Free

  • Multiple accounts with transfers.
  • Create transactions from templates
  • Two levels of categories (import from Grisbi XML)
  • Share export file (via email, FTP, Dropbox, ...)
  • Data backup and restore
  • Password protection
  • Export to QIF and CSV (MS Excel)
  • Calculator
  • Group transactions per day, week, month, year and display sums per group {% include new.html %}

Premium

The following features can be tried out five times for free and will be unlocked if you buy My Expenses Contrib :

  • Restore backups without uninstall/reinstall
  • Aggregate financial situation in account list
  • Editing of templates
  • Reset all accounts in batch
  • Security question for password loss
  • Cloning of transactions
  • Split transactions {% include new.html %}
