Summary

My Expenses is an Android app designed to keep track of your expenses and incomes, and to export them into a desktop finance tool, like Grisbi (Open Source), Gnucash (Open Source), MS Money, Quicken or Wiso Mein Geld.
Works on all Android versions from 1.6. to 4.2.

Features

  • Multiple accounts with transfers.
  • Create transactions from templates
  • Two levels of categories (import from Grisbi XML)
  • Share export file (via email, FTP, Dropbox, ...)
  • Data backup and restore
  • Password protection
  • Export to QIF and CSV (MS Excel)
