Privacy policy

My Expenses does not access any data on your device other than its own database and its own folder on the SD card. My Expenses does not send any data over the Internet. My Expenses uses the following permissions:

WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
This is needed for storing the exported QIF file on the SD card, and for creating database backups.
