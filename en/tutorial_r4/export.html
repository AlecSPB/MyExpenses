Export transactions

This document covers My Expenses until version 1.7. From version 1.8, My Expenses has integrated help dialogs, and no longer refers to an external user guide.

Finally, the raison d'être of My Expenses is its support for export into QIF format. Call this function through the Export and reset button from the transaction list. This will create a file on your SD card with all transactions for the current account, delete them from the database, and adjust the opening balance to the last current balance. My Expenses can share the file immediately after the export through FTP, email or any other file sharing app, as explained in Share export file.
Transfer operations will also be deleted from the account, but still be visible in the other account they refer to.

Voilà. You can now connect your phone to your computer, open Grisbi (or Gnucash), and use its import functionality.
