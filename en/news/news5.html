Password protection

2013-05-05

The feature most frequently requested by users in their comments on Google Play is password protection. It will be available with My Expenses 1.7.1 and can already be tested in the alpha version. The password can be set from the settings screen, and will be checked every time the app is started, or resumed after a delay of more than 10 seconds. The protection is global to the whole application, i.e. it covers not only the main transaction list, but all secondary screens (e.g. when you leave the app while editing an expense, selecting a category, or changing a configuration setting etc.).
