--- layout: default title: Privacy policy section: privacy --- My Expenses does not access any data on your device other than its own database and its own folder on the SD card. My Expenses does not send any data over the Internt. My Expenses uses the following permissions:
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
This is needed for storing the exported QIF file on the SD card, and for creating database backups.
INTERNET
This is needed for displaying the manual and the news (hosted as static pages by Github at http://myexpenses.totschnig.org) in a WebView from inside the app. Future versions will drop this permission and open these resources through the browser application.
